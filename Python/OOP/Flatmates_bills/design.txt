Title: Flatmates Bill
Description: An app that gets as input the amount of bill for a particular period and the days that each of the flatmates stayed in the house for that period and returns how much each flatmates has to pay. It is also generates a PDF report stating the names of the flatmates, the period, and how much each of them had to pay.

Object: Bill:
            amount
            period
        Flatmates:
            name
            days_in_house
            pays(bill)
        PdfReport:
            filename
            generate(flatmate1, flatmate2, bill)
